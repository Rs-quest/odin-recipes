Kapsalon

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 celery
  • 400 grams of sweet potato
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 350 grams of shawarma
  • 4 slices of cheese
  • 100 grams of iceberg lettuce, finely shredded
  • 100 grams cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 red onion in rings
  • 4 tablespoons of garlic
    • Optional additions
  • Jalapeño pepper, sliced
  • Sriracha

Steps

  1. Preheat your air fryer to 200°C (390°F)
  2. Clean the celery, cut it in half, remove the skin and root ends
  3. Slice the celery into 1 cm thick slices, then cut into fries
  4. Peel the sweet potatoes and cut them into similar-sized fries
  5. Toss both types of fries in olive oil and season with salt to taste
  6. Air fry for 12 minutes or until golden and crisp. Shake the basket halfway through for even cooking
  7. While the fries are cooking, heat a bit of oil in a pan and cook the shawarma over medium-high heat until nicely browned and cooked through.
  8. Divide the fries between four plates. Top with the cooked shawarma and a slice of cheese on each. Optional: place the plates (if oven-safe) under a hot grill or in the oven briefly to melt the cheese
  9. Finish with a handful of iceberg lettuce, cherry tomatoes, and red onion rings
  10. Drizzle with garlic sauce and garnish with sriracha and jalapeños to taste
