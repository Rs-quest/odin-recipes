Banana Oatmeal Cookies

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas (medium to large, well-spotted)
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/3 cup chocolate chips (or raisins, chopped nuts, or your favorite mix-in)
  • 1 tablespoon of peanut butter

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175°C (350°F) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper
  2. Mash the bananas in a medium bowl until smooth
  3. Add the oats and chocolate chips, and mix until everything is well combined
  4. Scoop tablespoon-sized amounts of the mixture onto the baking sheet and flatten slightly with the back of a spoon
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the cookies are set and lightly golden on the edges
  6. Cool on a wire rack and enjoy
